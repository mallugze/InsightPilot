# 🚀 InsightPilot – AI-Powered Decision Intelligence Platform

> **See Beyond the Numbers. Make Better Business Decisions with AI.**

InsightPilot is an AI-powered Decision Intelligence Platform that transforms spreadsheets into executive insights, business reports, and AI-driven recommendations in minutes.

Instead of manually creating dashboards or analyzing thousands of rows of data, InsightPilot acts as your personal AI Business Analyst—understanding your data, uncovering hidden opportunities, and helping organizations make confident, data-driven decisions.

# 💡 Why InsightPilot?

Organizations generate enormous amounts of business data every day, yet transforming that information into meaningful decisions remains one of the biggest challenges.

InsightPilot bridges this gap by combining deterministic business analytics with Google's Gemini AI to automatically explain trends, identify anomalies, generate executive summaries, and recommend actionable business strategies.

Rather than replacing traditional analytics, InsightPilot enhances it with conversational AI—allowing anyone to interact with their data as if speaking to an experienced business analyst.
