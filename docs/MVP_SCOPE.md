# InsightPilot — MVP Scope

Version: 0.1 Alpha

Status: Approved

---

# Objective

Build a production-quality Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that demonstrates how AI can transform business datasets into meaningful insights and actionable recommendations.

The objective is to deliver a complete end-to-end user experience rather than implementing every possible feature.

---

# Success Criteria

A user should be able to

Upload a dataset

↓

Understand the business within one minute

↓

Receive meaningful recommendations

↓

Ask follow-up questions

↓

Export an executive report

without requiring technical knowledge.

---

# Non-Goals

Do not build an enterprise BI platform.

Do not replicate Power BI.

Do not replicate Tableau.

Focus on solving the most important business problems with simplicity.
